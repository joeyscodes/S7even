The Vision

Where Art Meets Appetite

S7VEN isn’t just a restaurant — it’s a creative studio disguised as a dining room. Born in 2023 from the collaboration of Chef Kira Dos Santos and architect Luan Teixeira, every detail of S7VEN is intentional. From the curvature of the plates to the lighting that shifts with the evening, we orchestrate a journey through taste, sound, and texture.

Our menu changes with the seasons, always reflecting the freshest catch and local harvest. We believe in pushing boundaries while honoring the soul of Cape Verdean ingredients.

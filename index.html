About S7VEN

A New Era of Dining

Located in the vibrant heart of Praia, S7VEN redefines restaurant culture. We blend progressive techniques with locally sourced ingredients, creating plates that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. Our chefs are artists, our mixologists are alchemists — and your table is the canvas.

Ambient lighting, a curated soundtrack, and a sleek, minimalist interior set the stage for an unforgettable evening, whether you’re here for a romantic dinner or a stylish gathering.
